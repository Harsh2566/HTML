Paris

This blog is aimed at families: what to do, where to go, and fun things for the kids. Translation: how to keep the little ones happy and their parents sane, for the most part. Posts include reviews of places and activities in Paris, notices of upcoming events and sneak peeks into soon-to-arrive entertainment.

There’s information about everything under the sun pertaining to family fun: shopping, playgrounds, museums and so much more. Mama Loves Paris tweets daily and regularly posts in other social media avenues such as Facebook and Instagram.
